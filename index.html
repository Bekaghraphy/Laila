Rhythmic Gymnastics Intelligence

Rhythmic gymnastics originated in the early 20th century, blending classical ballet, expressive movement, and precise apparatus handling. Over decades, it evolved into a highly technical Olympic discipline, first appearing in the Games in 1984. Today, it represents a refined balance between athletic excellence and artistic expression.
