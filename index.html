A Brief History of Rhythmic Gymnastics

Rhythmic gymnastics originated in the early 20th century, combining elements of ballet, dance, and expressive movement. It evolved as a discipline focused on grace, musicality, and controlled body movement.

The sport gained international recognition in the mid-1900s and became part of the Olympic Games in 1984, continuously evolving in rules, apparatus, and artistic expression.
